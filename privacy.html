Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Sintbot ("we", "us") operates the website sintbot.com and the open-source software cc-bot. This policy explains what little data the site touches and how purchases are handled. We keep it deliberately minimal.

No accounts, no direct collection

The website has no login, no account system, and no forms. We do not directly collect names, emails, or other personal information when you browse the site.

Anonymous analytics

We use Umami for privacy-first, aggregate analytics. It sets no cookies, does not track you across sites, and does not collect personally identifiable information. We only see anonymous, aggregated stats such as page views and referrers.

Local storage

Your browser's localStorage is used only to remember your language preference (English or Chinese). Nothing is sent to us.

Purchases (Sintbot Pro)

When you subscribe to Sintbot Pro, payment is processed by Creem acting as our Merchant of Record. Your name, email, and payment details are submitted to and handled by Creem under their privacy policy: creem.io/privacy. We receive only the information needed to deliver support and manage your subscription.

The cc-bot software

The cc-bot software runs entirely on your own machine. It does not send your code, credentials, or data to us. It communicates only with the services you configure (such as your Slack or Lark workspace and the AI models you connect).

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@sintbot.com.

隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 6 月 12 日

Sintbot（「我们」）运营网站 sintbot.com 及开源软件 cc-bot。本政策说明本站接触到的极少量数据，以及购买流程如何处理。我们刻意把它做到最简。

无账号、不直接收集

本站没有登录、没有账号系统、没有表单。你浏览本站时，我们不会直接收集姓名、邮箱或其他个人信息。

匿名统计

我们使用 Umami 做隐私优先的聚合统计。它不设 cookie、不跨站追踪、不收集可识别个人身份的信息。我们只看到匿名的聚合数据，比如页面浏览量和来源。

本地存储

浏览器的 localStorage 仅用于记住你的语言偏好（英文或中文），不会向我们发送任何内容。

购买（Sintbot Pro）

订阅 Sintbot Pro 时，支付由 Creem 作为 Merchant of Record（卖方记录方）处理。你的姓名、邮箱和支付信息提交给 Creem，并按其隐私政策处理：creem.io/privacy。我们只接收提供支持和管理订阅所必需的信息。

cc-bot 软件

cc-bot 软件完全运行在你自己的机器上，不会把你的代码、凭证或数据传给我们。它只与你自己配置的服务通信（比如你的 Slack / 飞书 workspace 和你接入的 AI 模型）。

联系

有隐私方面的问题？请写信至 support@sintbot.com。
